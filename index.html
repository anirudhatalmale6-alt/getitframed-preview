Your Framing Partner

Printing, framing and more… all under one roof

Catering for everyone's needs from retail buyers to trade professionals

Operating locally for over 15 years, Get It Framed NI is a professional print & framing studio operating in the heart of Northern Ireland and shipping worldwide. Supplying prints, mounts, frames, canvases, albums and a variety of similar services and gifts; we cater for everyone from the home improver to photographers, artists and the business world.

The Studio Standard

Professional printing & framing services for public and trade.

We work closely within the trade sector and are the chosen framing partner for photographers and artists who need dependable production. That means consistent output across repeat orders, exhibitions, and client deliveries — with careful attention to borders, mounting alignment, and finish.

Because we print, mount, and frame under one roof, the details tie up: the print is sized for the mount, the mount sits perfectly in the frame, and the finished piece feels cohesive.

  • Consistent output across repeat orders
  • Accurate glass cutting and precise mounting
  • Careful attention to borders and finish
  • Studio standards for the general public
Our Print Studio

At Get It Framed NI, we believe your favourite memories, digital photography, and artwork deserve to live on walls, not just on screens and hard drives. From our dedicated print and framing studio in the heart of Portglenone, we transform your digital files into breathtaking, tangible prints.

Whether you are looking to print a single cherished family smartphone photo or need a full collection of exhibition-ready fine art reproductions, we treat every single image with the meticulous care and precision it deserves. Operating locally for over 15 years, we combine state-of-the-art print technology with a true passion for the craft, ensuring vibrant colours, rich blacks, and details that truly stand out.
